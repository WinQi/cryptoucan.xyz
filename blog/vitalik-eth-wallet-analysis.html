Who owns vitalik.eth?

Vitalik Buterin is the co-founder of Ethereum, the world's second-largest blockchain by market cap. He proposed Ethereum in 2013 at age 19, and the network went live in 2015. The address 0xd8dA6BF26964aF9D7eEd9e03E53415D37aA96045 — resolved by the ENS name vitalik.eth — is his publicly acknowledged primary wallet.

Unlike most crypto figures who keep their wallets private, Vitalik has been remarkably transparent. He has publicly shared this address for years, making it one of the most watched and analyzed wallets in the entire Ethereum ecosystem.

What does the on-chain data show?

As of early 2026, vitalik.eth holds a significant amount of ETH alongside a diverse collection of tokens — many of which were sent to him unsolicited by project teams hoping he would endorse or promote them.

Interesting pattern: Vitalik regularly donates large sums of tokens he receives to charitable causes. When projects airdrop their tokens to his address hoping for publicity, he often sends them directly to charity rather than selling — a behavior well-documented on-chain.
