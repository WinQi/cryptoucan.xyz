👋 General Feedback

Just want to tell us what you think of the product? Positive or negative — we appreciate honest feedback. It helps us build something people actually want to use.

⏱️ What to Expect

We're a small team, but we take every message seriously. Here's what you can generally expect after reaching out:

Bug reports and technical issues — we aim to acknowledge these within 24 hours and follow up with a fix or update as soon as possible. If you can include your browser, operating system, and the wallet address you were trying to scan, it helps us reproduce the issue faster.

Feature requests and general feedback — we read every one of these, even if we can't always reply individually. The most requested features have a strong influence on what we prioritise next.

Partnership and media inquiries — we'll get back to you within 1–3 business days. Please include as much context as possible about your project or publication so we can give you a useful response.

All data displayed on cryptoucan.xyz comes from the public Ethereum blockchain via Etherscan's API. If you notice a discrepancy between what we show and what you see on Etherscan directly, please include the wallet address and a screenshot — that information helps us identify and fix data processing issues quickly.
