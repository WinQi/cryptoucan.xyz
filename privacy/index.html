🌐 Website (cryptoucan.xyz)

When you use cryptoucan.xyz, any Ethereum wallet address you enter is used solely to query publicly available on-chain data via the Etherscan API. We do not store, log, or transmit any wallet addresses or search history on our servers. All data is fetched in real time and returned directly to your browser.

The Ethereum blockchain is a public ledger. All wallet data displayed on cryptoucan.xyz — including balances, transactions, and token holdings — is already publicly accessible to anyone in the world. We are making that existing public information easier to read.

No account creation is required. No wallet connection is needed. You can use cryptoucan.xyz completely anonymously.
